Welcome to the website

A website (also written as a web site) is a collection of web pages and related content
that is identified by a common domain name and published on at least one web server.


Websites are typically dedicated to a particular topic or purpose, such as news, education,
commerce, entertainment or social networking. Hyperlinking between web pages guides the


navigation of the site, which often starts with a home page. As of May 2023, the top 5 most
visited websites are Google Search, YouTube, Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram.
